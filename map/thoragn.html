Thoragn is the first planet of the Alcipid system.
She has a particularly dense and active atmosphere, which combined with her
closeness to the system start, makes for an intensely hostile environment.

Pre-scream records show significant military presence on Thoragn, but 
the required exploration of her surface to validate this is considered 
suicidal.
