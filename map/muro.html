Muro is a large terrestrial planet in the Allisto system best known for her
aggressively hostile biosphere. 

Muro's corrosive atmosphere is theorized to stem from some biological process,
but due to the great difficulty in accessing Muros ecology safely, no research
has been able to determine the exact origin.
