Anesin is a mostly uninhabited Desert world.

It has a sparse, but diverse biosphere, specially evolved for surviving the
the deadly heat and radiation of the twin Berin suns.

Some attempts at settlement are known to have happened, but no permanent
settlements have been recorded.
